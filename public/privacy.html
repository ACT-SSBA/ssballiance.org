Privacy Policy

This privacy policy defines how the information you submit via ssballiance.org will be used. Data is collected and stored via Reform.

Here are the types of data we collect and how they will be used:

  • Email: Your email address will only be used to keep you up to date on fixing Section 174. It will not be shared with any other organizations or used for other purposes.
  • Name: Your name will be used for signing the Coalition Letter. It otherwise will not be disclosed.
  • Company Name: Your name will be used for signing the Coalition Letter. It otherwise will not be disclosed.
  • Address: Your address will be used for determining which Congressional District you live in. Your full address will not be disclosed. We may reach out to you to help you reach out to your policymakers. Your Congressional District may be anonmyously disclosed to assist with this effort, but never combined with any identifying details. (For example, we may make a list of all Congressional districts where signers live.)
  • Impact: The free-form text you put in the box asking for the impact of Section 174 on you will only be used in an anonymized manner.
